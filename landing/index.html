One platform · two surfaces

Built for both sides of the conversation.

For mothers

Hera, a calm companion.

Tell Hera what's happening. She asks the right follow-ups, gives guidance from your clinic's playbook (never the open web), and escalates to a real human when it matters.

  • Soft check-ins for mood, energy, pain
  • Cute persona views for pregnancy and baby
  • Calm meditation companion + care plan
  • One tap to your care team

For clinics

A prioritized care queue.

Patient messages arrive ranked by acuity, summarized, with full timeline and recommended next step. Routine asks resolve on protocol. Your team only sees what needs a human.

  • Risk-ranked queue · Red / Orange / Yellow / Green / Gray
  • Explainable triage with citations on every decision
  • One-click chart-ready summary
  • Weekly ROI: hours saved, leakage recovered

How it fits

Structure between visits. Escalate only what matters.

  1. 01

    Patients check in, gently.

    Mom and baby check-ins around recovery, mood, feeding, sleep, and symptoms. Three minutes, no clinical jargon, no chasing.

  2. 02

    Hera summarizes and ranks.

    Every conversation becomes a structured triage decision with a reason, a recommended action, and the source it came from.

  3. 03

    Low-acuity resolves on protocol.

    Routine reassurance, normal-range guidance, and baby-care coaching are handled in-app from your clinic's playbook. Hours back.

  4. 04

    The right cases reach the right clinician.

    Urgent or unclear cases arrive in your queue with context, baseline deviation, evidence, and a suggested next step.

Trust + safety

Explainable, auditable, never autonomous.

Source-bound, not open-web.

Hera retrieves from ACOG, AAP, ABM, peer-reviewed maternal studies, and your clinic's protocols. Every answer cites its source.

Clinicians stay the decision-maker.

Hera classifies, ranks, routes, and drafts. She does not diagnose. Clinician feedback on every routing retrains the system.

Private by design.

HIPAA-aligned. Role-based access. Audit trails on every action. Consent-based data sharing. Clinic-owned workflows.
